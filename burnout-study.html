Occupational burnout study
in polish gamedev industry 2024

This report presents the first comprehensive study on burnout and job satisfaction in Poland's game development industry. Conducted in April-May 2024, the survey gathered responses from 223 professionals across various roles and employment types. The research examines factors such as gender, specialization, work mode, and career stage, providing a detailed snapshot of the current state of the Polish gamedev workforce.
